Our Mission

We are dedicated to advancing cybersecurity education through the OWASP methodology. Our consortium brings together industry experts, developers, and security professionals to create comprehensive training programs and foster a global community committed to secure software development.

From intensive workshops to hands-on labs, we equip participants with practical skills to identify, prevent, and mitigate the latest security threats.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is OWASP?

OWASP (Open Web Application Security Project) is a nonprofit foundation that works to improve the security of software. We're dedicated to providing unbiased, practical information.

Who should attend training?

Developers, security engineers, DevOps professionals, and anyone involved in building or securing web applications. No prior cybersecurity experience required.

Are certificates provided?

Yes! All participants receive OWASP Consortium certificates of completion recognized by industry leaders.
